Part 2 — Customer Schedules Pickup

Customer Experience
3
Welcome screen

Shows "Good News, Jacob!" with their order number, a product preview image, and pickup dates (Apr 16–18). They tap "Select My Pickup Time".

4
Time slot selection

Grid shows Thursday, Friday, Saturday with 30-minute slots. Color-coded: green (open), yellow (filling), red (full). Customer picks a slot and confirms.

5
Confirmation / Receipt card

After confirming, the page becomes their pickup receipt:

  • Warehouse label (e.g. "B01") — big black box at top
  • QR code — for staff to scan at check-in
  • Their name + order number
  • Pickup date/time
  • Directions button → opens Google Maps
  • Add to Calendar button → downloads .ics file
  • Map of warehouse location
  • Their items list

Part 4 — Day-of Operations

Staff Experience
9
Customer arrives at warehouse

They show their phone with the QR code on the receipt page.

10
Staff scans QR code

Opens the scan page on your phone. Shows the customer's label, name, items, and a big "Check In" button. Tap to check them in.

11
Staging team pulls items

Use the warehouse label (e.g. "B01") to locate pre-staged items. The label tells you the staging zone (Dock for benches, Floor for seats, etc.)

12
Load and complete

Help customer load. Tap "Mark Complete" on the scan page. Done!

Testing Checklist

Please verify each of these and report any issues.

Email

  • Email arrives with correct subject line and preview text
  • Customer name is personalized ("Hey Jacob,")
  • Items table shows correct items and quantities
  • Red "Select My Pickup Time" button links to correct page
  • Pickup dates and location are correct (Apr 16–18, Unit 4, Roca)
  • Email renders well on mobile (check on your phone)

Customer Pickup Flow

  • Welcome page loads with customer name and order number
  • Time slot grid shows all days with correct hours
  • Can select a time slot and confirm
  • Confirmation page shows label, QR code, name, order number
  • Directions button opens Google Maps to correct address
  • Add to Calendar downloads valid .ics file
  • QR code is scannable (try with phone camera)
  • Page looks good on mobile — receipt card is above the fold

Admin

  • Dashboard shows time slot heatmap with all extended hours
  • Customer detail page loads correctly for any customer
  • Preview Receipt shows phone-frame mockup
  • Scan page loads when QR code is scanned
  • Check In and Mark Complete buttons work
  • Day-of Ops shows staging groups by time slot
